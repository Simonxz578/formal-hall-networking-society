Home / About

A formal setting, a relaxed conversation.

The society brings together Cambridge students who enjoy formal hall culture, interdisciplinary conversation and meeting people from different colleges, years and backgrounds.

We organise regular cross-college formal swaps, themed networking dinners and informal meet-ups, often in collaboration with JCRs, MCRs, cultural societies and alumni groups.

Our events emphasise inclusive, relaxed conversation: not transactional networking, but meaningful dialogue that can lead to lasting friendships, collaborations and professional connections.

Home / Values

Home / FAQ

Questions before joining?

These answers are intended for the first version of the website and can be updated as the committee confirms future event arrangements.

Who can join?

The society is primarily for Cambridge students. Some events may also welcome alumni, guests or collaborators depending on capacity and venue rules.

Is this an official University of Cambridge website?

No. This website is operated by the Formal Hall Networking Society. It is not an official University of Cambridge website unless otherwise stated.

What is the dress code?

Dress code depends on the event and college. Formal halls usually require smart dress, and some colleges may require gowns for members of that college.

How are events announced?

Events are announced through the mailing list and official society channels. Please subscribe to soc-fhn and confirm your subscription by email.

Can guests attend?

Guest attendance depends on the event format, venue capacity and committee policy. Details will be included in each event announcement.
